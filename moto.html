Detailed 3D modeling of a unicorn-themed motorcycle combining creativity and technical precision.

The design

We aimed to create a playful and fantastical motorcycle design, featuring a unicorn theme and vivid colors. Starting with the unicorn as the front fairing, we developed a visually engaging concept combining imaginative elements with mechanical function. The use of spring shapes in the rear wheel evokes the unicorn's horn, tying the theme together.

Rear Wheel and Spring Integration

The rear wheel design involved advanced modeling techniques. Using sweep operations and parametric laws, we crafted helicoidal springs with varying radius to serve both aesthetic and structural purposes. Multiple B√©zier curves and multi-section surfaces shaped the wheel, while circular patterns were used to duplicate the springs evenly.

Front Fairing and Unicorn Horn

The front fairing includes a sculpted unicorn head and horn, achieved with complex surface operations and sweep functions. Defining multiple planes for curve projections was crucial. We also added a 80mm pocket to embed a headlight, blending design with utility.

Front Fender Shaping

The fender design required accurate alignment with the wheel. We used splines and multi-surface tools to wrap the geometry around the tire and brake disk. Precise transformations were needed to properly align the given profile to the tilted orientation of the wheel.
